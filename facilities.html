Facilities

Our school is equipped with a wide range of modern facilities to provide an enriching environment for both academic and extracurricular growth. Explore the various facilities we offer to enhance student life.

  1. Home
  2. Facilities

Computer Lab

Our state-of-the-art computer lab is equipped with the latest technology and software to help students develop essential IT skills for the digital age.

Science Lab

Our science labs are fully equipped for practical experiments in physics, chemistry, and biology, offering hands-on learning experiences.

Sports Facilities

We provide a variety of sports facilities, including basketball courts, a football field, and a gymnasium to promote physical fitness and teamwork.

Tours and Travels

Our school organizes educational and recreational tours to various locations, providing students with opportunities to explore and learn outside the classroom.

Cultural Programs

Our school hosts various cultural programs throughout the year, where students showcase their talents in music, dance, drama, and more, promoting creativity and cultural awareness.

Introducing with Nature

Our school encourages students to connect with nature through outdoor activities, gardening projects, and environmental awareness programs to foster a sense of responsibility for the planet.
