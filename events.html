Events

Our school organizes various events throughout the year, including academic competitions, cultural festivals, sports meets, and more. Join us in celebrating creativity, learning, and teamwork!

  1. Home
  2. Events

Annual Sports Day

Our Annual Sports Day brings together students for a day of friendly competition, physical fitness, and team spirit. Events include track and field, relay races, and more.

Cultural Festival

Our Cultural Festival celebrates the diversity and creativity of our students with performances in music, dance, drama, and art.

Science Exhibition

Our Science Exhibition allows students to showcase their innovative projects and experiments in various scientific fields, promoting curiosity and knowledge.

Educational Tour

Our educational tours offer students the opportunity to visit historical sites, museums, and cultural landmarks, expanding their knowledge beyond the classroom.

Talent Show

Our Talent Show is a platform for students to display their talents in singing, dancing, acting, and more. It encourages creativity and self-expression.
