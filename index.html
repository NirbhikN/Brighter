Welcome to Brighter English School

Brighter English School is dedicated to providing quality education that prepares students for success in school and in life. We believe education should inspire and equip students with the skills they need for the future.

  • Modern teaching methods that make learning fun and effective.
  • Supportive teachers who care about every student's growth.
  • A safe and welcoming environment where students can thrive.
  • Strong focus on building language, communication, and critical thinking skills.
  • Opportunities for extracurricular activities that promote personal development.
  • Commitment to preparing students for future challenges and opportunities.

Namaste and Welcome to Brighter English School!

I am Bhaskar Bhattarai, the principal of Brighter English School, and I am delighted to extend a warm welcome to all our students and their families. We are proud to offer an environment where education is not just about books but also about building character, creativity, and confidence.

At Brighter English School, we focus on holistic development, ensuring that each child receives the attention and encouragement they need to excel. Our dedicated staff is committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future. We believe in fostering a culture of learning that celebrates curiosity and fosters resilience.

We invite you to become part of our family and experience the difference that a truly supportive, empowering, and caring educational community can make. I look forward to seeing each of our students thrive and achieve their dreams!

- Bhaskar Bhattarai, Principal
